About VANBA
Founded by: Vineet Pradhan
Team: Aryan Khanzode, Abhishek Verma, Bhavik Bhalekar, Niraj Nishad , Rashmita Das
In 2025, a 20-year-old developer named Vineet Pradhan launched something extraordinary. But he didn’t do it alone. Surrounded by four close friends name Aryan Khanzode, Abhishek Verma, Bhavik Bhalekar, Niraj Nishad who shared his vision, Vineet turned what started as a late-night idea into a full-fledged company: VANBA — a name derived from "VANBAi", meaning "to build" — because VANBA was never just about code; it was about building something real, together.
Our Platform
VANBA's AI Interview Simulator is designed to help job seekers master their interview skills in a real-time, interactive environment. Our platform offers:
  • Real-time AI-powered interview room with voice and timer logic
  • Plan-based features: Basic (15 min) and Premium (35 min) interviews
  • Unlimited auto-generated, personalized questions
  • Auto-advance and robust controls for seamless experience
  • Premium activation system with timed access and lockout
  • Live feedback, emotion detection, and performance analytics
  • Responsive design for mobile, tablet, and PC
  • Profile management and progress tracking
  • Client reviews and top feedback highlights
  • Easy access to support and About Us information
Every page and feature is built to empower you to practice, improve, and succeed in your career journey. Welcome to VANBA — where we build your future, together.
